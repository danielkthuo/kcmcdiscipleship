Partner Updates

Stories of transformation, testimonies, and highlights of the children you support

Child of the Month

Maria Santos, Age 8

Brazil Sponsored since 2020

Maria joined our program three years ago when her family was struggling to make ends meet. Despite facing challenges, Maria has excelled in her studies and is now at the top of her class.

Her teacher says, "Maria is a bright, determined student with a heart of gold. She often helps her classmates who are struggling with their lessons."

Maria dreams of becoming a doctor one day so she can help others in her community.

Stories of Transformation

From Struggles to Success

Kenya

David joined our program five years ago when he lost both parents. Through the support of partners like you, he not only completed his education but is now enrolled in a vocational training program for automotive repair.

"Without Hope Springs Ministry, I don't know where I would be today. They gave me hope and a future," David shares.

A New Beginning

Philippines

When Ana's family home was destroyed in a typhoon, Hope Springs Ministry provided emergency shelter and helped rebuild their lives. Today, Ana's family has a safe home, and she is thriving in school.

"We lost everything, but we found hope through this ministry. They stood with us in our darkest hour," says Ana's mother.
