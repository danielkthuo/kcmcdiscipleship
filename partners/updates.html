Frequently Asked Questions

How much of my donation goes directly to programs?

85% of all donations go directly to our programs supporting children. Only 15% is used for administrative and fundraising costs, which is well below the industry average.

Can I choose which country my donation supports?

Yes! When you become a partner, you can specify if you'd like your donation to support programs in a particular country or allow us to allocate it where it's most needed.

How will I receive updates about my sponsored child?

You'll receive quarterly updates including photos, letters, and progress reports. You can also communicate directly with your sponsored child through our secure portal.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Hope Springs Ministry is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
