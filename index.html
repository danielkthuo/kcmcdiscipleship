About Our Discipleship Programs

At Kingdom Covenant Ministries, we believe in nurturing spiritual growth at every stage of life. Our discipleship programs are designed to help believers grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ by walking in the light of His truth.

"Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path."

Psalm 119:105

Our programs guide you step by step to understand who Jesus is, what it means to follow Him, and how to live a life that honors God. Through our discipleship journeys, you will learn important truths from the Bible, enrich your prayer life, discover your identity in Christ, grow in God's character, and serve others with your gifts.

Whether you're beginning your faith journey or seeking to deepen your walk with God, our programs provide the structure, community, and biblical foundation needed for spiritual growth and transformation.

Womb to Worship Program

Nurturing Your Child's Spiritual Journey From the Very Beginning

Transform your pregnancy and early parenting journey into a sacred experience. Our biblically-based curriculum helps you create a God-centered environment that shapes your child's spiritual foundation even before birth.

VISION

To help parents spiritually nurture their child from pregnancy/birth to age 2 by establishing faith habits, developing godly character, and building deep spiritual relationships during early childhood — our critical formative period for life.

PROGRAM PURPOSE

We believe that parents, caregivers, and ministry leaders should understand that discipleship is a journey starting before birth. Through monthly spiritual prompting, practical tools, and age-appropriate faith activities, this two-year journey will prepare families to:

  1. Speak God's Word and blessing over their unborn/newborn children.
  2. Introduce spiritual rhythms and Biblical values as habits from day one.
  3. Create a Christ-centered environment in the home.
  4. Refer to parenting as an intentional effort to model godly behavior and love through consistency.
  5. Recognize that young children can still experience the love, peace, and presence of God — even before they can talk.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start the Womb to Worship curriculum?

You can begin at any stage of pregnancy or after your child is born. The curriculum is designed to be flexible and adapt to your family's journey.

How much time does the program require each day?

The activities are designed to fit into your daily routine, requiring just 10-15 minutes of focused time each day, with spiritual practices integrated throughout your day.

Is this program only for first-time parents?

Not at all! The curriculum is valuable for parents at any stage, whether you're expecting your first child or adding to your growing family.

What age group is the PathLight program for?

The PathLight program is designed for teens and youth aged 13-18, focusing on building a strong biblical foundation during these formative years.
