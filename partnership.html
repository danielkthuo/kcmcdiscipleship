About Kingdom Covenant Ministries Church

Kingdom Covenant Ministries Church (KCMC) has a central focus, Christ-centered, and Bible-oriented ministry intentional about spiritual transformation and community impact alongside leadership advancement.

Child & Youth Ministry Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To see children & youths come to know Christ and be Biblically discipled; to consistently grow in Christ and become fulfilled Christian adults.

Our Vision: To reach into the lives of children & youth in our church and community with the Good News, portray the love of Christ, and teach them the Word of God.

"Train up a child in the way he should go and when he is old, he will not depart from it." - Proverbs 22:6

Our Programs

  • Children and Youth Discipleship - creating spiritual growth and leadership in early life
  • Evangelism and Missions - assisting the lost and recreating them with the Gospel in local and regional ways
  • Educational and biblical teaching - providing believers with the equipment they need to mature and minister
  • Leadership Development - putting people in position with development in mind for ethical, moral, and purpose-driven leaders
  • Community Empowerment - solving practical social, educational, and economic community challenges

Our Commitment to Stewardship

At KCMC, we take stewardship seriously and will be intentional, transparent, and accountable, as we seek to steward every opportunity and resource in integrity and excellence. We take seriously our strategic partners. We consider them the means to grow our influence and capacity.

Joining as a partner with KCMC means supporting a more prolonged transformation that is spiritual, practical, social, and generational.

We look forward to working with people, churches, and organizations who want a partner that has the same vision of advancing the Kingdom of God and serving the community and making a practical difference.
